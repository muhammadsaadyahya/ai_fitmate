AI FitMate is an intelligent AI-powered fitness assistant designed to provide personalized workout guidance, real-time exercise correction, and smart fitness tracking. The application uses computer vision and artificial intelligence to analyze users’ body movements through camera-based pose detection, evaluate exercise form, and provide instant feedback to help users perform workouts safely and effectively.

The app acts as a virtual fitness trainer by detecting incorrect postures, counting repetitions, monitoring exercise accuracy, and giving voice-based guidance to improve performance. AI FitMate also generates personalized workout recommendations based on user goals, fitness level, and progress.

With advanced AI capabilities, including pose estimation and adaptive visual guidance, AI FitMate helps users understand the correct exercise techniques by providing real-time posture comparisons and feedback. The goal of the application is to make professional-level fitness coaching accessible anytime and anywhere while reducing the risk of injuries caused by incorrect workout forms.

AI FitMate combines artificial intelligence, computer vision, and fitness technology to create an interactive and personalized training experience for users of all fitness levels.
